Version 1.1.0
-------------

New Features

- Copilot Assistant: Your new AI-powered coding companion.
- Open Settings Command: Easily access and your settings.
- About Command: Quickly get information about the package.
- Enhanced Rendering for Ask Command: Enjoy a smoother and more intuitive experience.
- Right-Click to Generate Shareable Link**: Share your work effortlessly.
- Buttons in the snippet sheet: Added 'Copy' 'Edit','Share' and 'Delete' buttons. 
- New Onboarding Flow: Get started faster with our improved onboarding process.


Removed

- Deprecated Keybindings: Removed the `ctrl+c` `delete` and `ctrl+e` keybindings.
